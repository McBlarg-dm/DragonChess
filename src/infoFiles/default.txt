You control the capital characters
and the computer controls the lower.

Moves take the form of "pcr to pcr"
where p is the plane, c is the column
letter, and r is the row number.

Planes:(S)ky, (G)round, (C)ave
Columns: a-l
Rows: 1-8

Useful Commands:
/p - Show rules for piece
/winning - Shows who the board favors
/back
